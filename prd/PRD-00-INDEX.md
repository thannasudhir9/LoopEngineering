## How to Use

Each PRD contains:
- **Goal** — what this feature achieves and why
- **User Stories** — who needs it and what they do
- **Tasks** — implementation checklist with subtasks
- **Acceptance Criteria** — definition of done
- **Dependencies** — what must exist first
